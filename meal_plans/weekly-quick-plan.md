## Recipes

### Monday ‚Äî Spicy Gochujang Chicken Stir-Fry With Cauliflower Rice
*20 min ¬∑ ~480 kcal*

1. Crush garlic and fry in sesame oil for 30 sec.
2. Add chicken breast strips and cook through (~5 min).
3. Add shredded veg mix and sweet pea pods, stir-fry 2 min.
4. Mix gochujang paste, tomato paste, and soy sauce ‚Äî pour over and toss.
5. Microwave or pan-fry cauliflower rice until hot, serve alongside.

---

### Tuesday ‚Äî Sticky Black Pepper Steak With Sweet Chilli & Garlic Pak Choi
*10 min ¬∑ ~550 kcal*

1. Season steak generously with cracked black pepper.
2. Sear in a very hot pan 2‚Äì3 min each side (medium-rare). Rest 2 min, then slice.
3. In the same pan, fry roasted garlic paste 30 sec, add pak choi and stir-fry 2 min.
4. Deglaze with Chinese rice wine, add hoisin sauce and red chilli relish ‚Äî stir to coat.
5. Serve steak over cooked rice with pak choi and sticky sauce spooned over.

---

### Wednesday ‚Äî 10-Min Chicken Fried Rice With Mange Tout
*10 min ¬∑ 373 kcal ¬∑ 37g protein*

1. Stir-fry garlic paste and chilli in a hot pan for 30 sec.
2. Add diced chicken, cook 4 min.
3. Add mange tout, spinach, and steamed rice ‚Äî stir-fry 2 min.
4. Splash in fish sauce, toss through basil and spring onions.

---

### Thursday ‚Äî Lamb Mince Kati Rolls & Coriander Chutney
*25 min ¬∑ ~620 kcal*

1. Fry garlic, ginger, and curry powder in a pan for 1 min.
2. Add lamb mince, breaking it apart ‚Äî cook until browned (~8 min).
3. Season with chilli flakes, squeeze of lemon.
4. Mix mayo with remaining coriander to make the chutney sauce.
5. Warm tortillas, fill with lamb mince, sliced tomato, gem lettuce, red onion, and drizzle with chutney.
